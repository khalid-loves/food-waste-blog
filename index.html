Tips to reduce Food Waste.

1.Plan weekly meals in advance and make a shopping list to reduce the chance of buying extra food you don't need.

2.Keep food fresh longer with proper storage techniques and food placement.

3.Use food that's already in your fridge to create recipes instead of always buying new.

4.Compost remaining food scraps either through a backyard composter or curbside collection program(if available).
